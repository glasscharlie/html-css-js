Alpacas

https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b9/Llama_lying_down.jpg/330px-Llama_lying_down.jpg

There are only two breeds of alpacas, huacaya and suri. Alpacas weigh between 121 and 143 pounds, and are bbetween 4 to 7 feet long. Alpacas are native to the Andes, at elevations up to 15,750 feet, though they are very adaptable. Alpacas are herbavores, they only eat vegetation. They breed once a year, it takes 242 to 365 days for an alpaca to give birth after the gestation period starts. The baby alpaca (called a crea) weights 18 to 20 pounds when it is born.
